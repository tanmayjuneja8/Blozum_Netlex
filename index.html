Learn more about netLex!

netLex is Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM) software. The platform is used by market-leading companies to efficiently manage not only contracts, but also powers of attorney, legal consultations, corporate acts and other documents.

Various sectors of large companies, such as Legal, Commercial, Purchasing, Finance and others, can use netLex to create files, send them for approval and signature, negotiate them with customers or suppliers, store them automatically, as well as to extract data to build management dashboards and graphics for intelligence.

Contract Lifecycle Management (CLM) is the set of management practices that organizes all the stages that contracts go through between their preparation and fulfillment.

These steps may involve, for example: requesting the creation of the document, preparing the text, negotiating with the client or supplier, approving the final version, signing and formalizing it, fulfilling obligations and, finally, ending or renewing it.

netLex is CLM software that adds technology to these management practices, centralizing the entire lifecycle of contracts in a simple and secure way.
